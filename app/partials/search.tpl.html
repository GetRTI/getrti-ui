Do you want to file an RTI? Don't know who to address your RTI application to?

The Public Information Officer (PIO) is the Central Point of Contact (POC) for filing applications under the Right to Information (RTI). The PIO is also responsible for providing the requested information to the Information Seeker. Thus the role of the PIO is very critical for the success of the RTI act.

Citizens often find it very difficult to the find the relevant PIO for a particular public authority. This discourages a lot of them from using the RTI act. This application allows you to search for the relevant PIO for filing the RTI both for Central Government and State Governments. The data is collated from various government sources.
