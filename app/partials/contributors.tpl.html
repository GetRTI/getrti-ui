Meet the contributors!

Bhanupriya Rao

Bhanupriya is a campaigner for governance transparency and accountability and Open Government systems. She has campaigned for Right to Food and Right to Information in India and worked on the 'Economic Literacy and Budget Accountability' program in around thirty countries in Asia and Africa. She has worked with Center for Equity Studies, Oxfam and ActionAid International.

She is currently the Regional Coordinator of Asia and Latin America region for the Open Data Barometer developed by the Web Foundation, the first ever worldwide study on the state of Open Governmentt Data in 85 countries.

She has a Masters and Mphil in History from Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi and an Msc in Public Policy from School of Oriental and African Studies(SOAS), London.

Rakesh Dubbudu

Rakesh Dubbudu has a bachelors degree in Electronics and Communications Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Warangal (formerly REC Warangal). He is presently Co-Convenor of National Campaign for People‟s Right to Information (NCPRI) and also the Co-Convenor of United Forum for RTI Campaign – AP. He is also the AP state Co-ordinator of National Election Watch(NEW).

He trains people on the Right to Information and has trained close to 10000 people so far. He is also a RTI trainer to Department of Rural Development, Govt of AP. He also works with various Government departments to help improve their RTI complaince.
